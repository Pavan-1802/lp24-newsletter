IEEE in Collaboration with GTech MuLearn set for Launchpad 2024 Job Fair

May 31, 2024

Launchpad Kerala 2024 - Connecting Talent with Opportunities

About Launchpad Kerala

Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier event organized by the IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. This event aims to create opportunities for engineering students and graduates by connecting them with pioneering companies in the technical and engineering sectors. It strives to foster connections that drive progress and innovation within Kerala's job market.

Launchpad Kerala Highlights

Unique Assessment Approach

The event features a distinct assessment process involving a rigorous technical challenge and online tests. This comprehensive evaluation of both technical and non-technical skills ensures that only the most qualified candidates advance to interviews, offering unmatched opportunities for both participants and companies.

Flexible Interview Locations

Shortlisted candidates can choose to attend interviews in Trivandrum, Kochi, or Kozhikode. This flexibility simplifies the hiring process for companies and maximizes participation from top talent throughout the state.

Comprehensive Talent Pool

Launchpad Kerala 2024 presents a diverse talent pool from across Kerala, catering to various skill sets and experience levels. This gives companies access to top-tier candidates for a variety of roles.

Strategic Partnerships

The collaboration between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn amplifies the impact of Launchpad Kerala 2024. This partnership ensures that candidates undergo thorough assessments while providing companies access to top talent and enhanced brand visibility.

Benefits for Recruiters

Enhanced Brand Visibility

Recruiters can distinguish themselves among competitors by showcasing their brand's ethos and career opportunities to top talent, establishing their status as a preferred employer in the industry.

Cost-Efficient Recruitment Solutions

By participating in Launchpad Kerala, companies can significantly reduce recruitment costs and streamline their hiring processes, ensuring optimal resource allocation while accessing Kerala's best talent.

Access to Elite Talent

Recruiters gain the opportunity to engage with a diverse pool of top-tier professionals, graduates, and emerging talent, equipping their organizations with the skills needed for sustainable growth and innovation.

Talent Pipeline Enrichment

Companies can develop a future-ready workforce by nurturing relationships with promising candidates, offering internships, and fostering professional development initiatives to ensure a continuous flow of skilled talent ready to drive success.

Benefits for Students

Networking Opportunities

Students have the chance to network with professionals and representatives from various innovative companies, establishing valuable connections for potential internships, co-op opportunities, and future employment.

Access to Industry Insights

Attending the job fair exposes students to the latest trends, technologies, and challenges in their field. They can gain insights into industry best practices, upcoming projects, and the skills in demand by interacting directly with industry professionals.

Exposure to Diverse Career Paths

Launchpad Kerala features a wide range of companies across different sectors and industries, exposing students to diverse career paths they may not have considered before. This helps them explore various options and find the right fit for their interests and skills.

Personal Development

Participating in Launchpad Kerala 2024 offers students a transformative experience that fosters personal growth and development. Through the assessment process, students push their limits, embrace new opportunities, and cultivate resilience, adaptability, and self-confidence.
